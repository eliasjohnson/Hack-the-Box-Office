Books

A book, a book, my kingdom for a book, or something like that. The longing feeling of wishing that there was more when you come to the end of a book that kept you captivated. A deep sense of unease at the thoughts and actions of an entirely fictional character, or pure joy at two imagined beings' love story. Books that have it all.

We have been at the receiving end of a book recommendation, and the giving end too. Nothing seems to bore you more than feeling like you have to read a book you don't want too.

Books should be fun, you should feel like you cant put it down. We read, and re-read the ones we love, and having a book make its way into your heart make you all the more inclined to recommend it.

Here we will discuss literature, from fantasy to non-fiction, and give you the best reasons as to why you should care.
