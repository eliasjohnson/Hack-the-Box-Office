Have you ever watched a movie and felt like it was a complete waste of time?
Have you ever left the theater with your friend and said "Well that sucked!" only for them to say, "I don't know, I kinda liked it."

You ask why, incredulously, ready with all the plot holes and discrepancies that have now become bullets to shoot holes in your friend's defense of enjoyment.
Part of what makes Movies, or any media worth talking about is the fact that two people, with slightly different perspectives can take completely different things away from a story.
It's not your fault a coming of age story isn't relatable at 65, any more that you could fault a child for not understanding the emotional implications of a midlife crisis at the young age of 8.
