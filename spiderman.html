Spider-Man

2002

Starring: Tobey Maguire, Willem Dafoe,
Kirsten Dunst, and James Franco
Rating: 7.4/10

After being bitten by a genetically-modified spider, a shy teenager gains spider-like abilities that he uses to fight injustice as a masked superhero and face a vengeful enemy.
This movie shaped my childhood, words cannot describe the way it changed my mind. I tried to climb on walls and sling webs, I never enjoyed princess or barbie movies the same. I can truly say that Spider-Man was the start of my commic addiction.
To me, this is Spider-Man!!!!
Whenever I think of Spider-Man, I think of this film!!!
Spider-Man is my all time favourite Marvel character!
Everything about this film feels right in my opinion.
From the amazing casting, the action set pieces, the practical effects, the story, the emotion and the overall tone of the film!!
Tobey Maguire's portrayal of Peter Parker/Spider-Man brings depth and charm to the character, making him relatable and endearing. The movie showcases the origin story of Spider-Man with great attention to detail, blending action and heartwarming moments seamlessly. Willem Dafoe's performance as the menacing Green Goblin is memorable and adds a compelling antagonist to the narrative.
